Haleigh's Favorite Foods

I love trying out new foods and new places! I also made it my New Years resolution to become a better cook so I've been trying out new dishes. I'm hoping to learn more throughout the year and to try new places.

Food I've Made Recently

Banana Bread

This is a banana bread I made recently. This was my first time and it was so good. I had some overripe bananas and substituted some flour for coca powder and added extra chocolate chips.

Fall Harvest Sheet Pan

This recipe is a sheet pan of butternut squash, chicken sausage, and brussel sprouts. With this meal all you have to do is chop and season. I like to toss them in oil, salt, paprica and onion salt and put them in the oven. I make it all the time.

Sweet Potato Nachos

I've made this meal twice so far this year and it's delicious! You cut sweet potatoes into smaller slices and spread them across the pan. Then I cooked ground turkey and corn and spread it across the top. Then I added olives, avocado, and sour cream across the top. I highly recommend it!

New Places I've Gone To Eat

1847 at the Stamm House

The first new place I tried out this year was The Stamm House! It's over in Middleton and has a wide menu with anything from burgers to pasta to fish fry. I went for my birthday and got the pesto peperadelle. It was amazing.

Gates and Brovi

Next is Gates and Brovi. This place had such a cool inside and their food was so good. I will be going back. I ordered their breakfast sandwich and it was quite delicious.

Butterbird

I had been wanting to try Butterbird for so long and it did not disappoint! I was there during their brunch time and their chicken and waffles were amazing. The vibes inside also were a 10/10.

Madison Sourdough

Can you tell I like breakfast food? Madison Sourdough is a must if you live in the area. Their croissant breakfast sandwich was 10/10 and their pastries and bread looked delicious. I will be back soon.
